Matt Jackson

With a life-long insatiable pursuit of knowledge & strength, Matt founded GymApp in 2016. That same pursuit led Matt to achieving his goals of an elite-level status powerlifter, professional strongman, founder/owner of the Powerlifting Gym of the Year in 2013 & 2014, long-time respected coach, and now founder/ CEO of GymApp.

Josh Armstrong

Josh is our Chief Information Officer. He has worked in marketing and communications since 2007, including in-house corporate marketing and running his own agency. After having GymApp as a client and supporting the team externally since its inception, Josh officially joined the Barbell Logic leadership team in May of 2020.

In addition to his work in marketing, Josh serves as Executive Director of Go Shout Love, a nonprofit organization that raises funds for families with children on rare medical journeys.
